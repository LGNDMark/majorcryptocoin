Cup and Handle Analysis (BDL, SPR, GEO)

People are often concerned with their portfolio growth - especially when missing out on lost opportunities. There is no way to predict the market with perfect accuracy. But there are many strategies that technical experts swear by. Today we will be introducing you to the cup and handle pattern. This simple indicator that is easy spot often leads to low market cap coins booming!

Below shows the recent activity BitDeal (BDL). You can see the cup and handle pattern that has formed followed by a steep increase in price. This one isn’t over yet. It should experience a continued rise in price over the next month.

Contributors: Adam Hirsch 

6 Month Look at BDL

Zooming out shows a large wedge starting in late september. It consolidated to mid December giving rise to the cup and handle. This may add more momentum to the cup and handle!

GEO/BTC

Geocoin formed a cup and handle pattern that led to an impulse several months ago as seen to the left of the chart.  It’s bound to repeat the same pattern. The results will of course be different, but the cup and handle suggest a large increase in price is coming very soon.
