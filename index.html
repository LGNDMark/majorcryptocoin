Myriad Coin. A coin for Everyone.

To start us off on our maiden voyage of crypto bloggin’ we are featuring Myriad Coin. We will be talking about its strengths, opportunities for growth and more. We think its a great potential investment for the HODL! By the end of this post we think you might feel the same.

How is it Different?

Myriad Coin is a cryptocurrency flying under the radar at 283rd place for total market cap, according to coinmarketcap.com. It uses what is called a multi-algo system, using 5 different algorithms. Myriad coin was the first crypto to do this and believes that this strategy will have a huge impact on the stability and fairness of the coin.
Why is that? Because one of the major issues in the crypto world is pump and dumps caused by large sells by whales with large sums of coin. This may be done for ‘political’ reasons within the developer communities(see the Bitcoin vs. Bitcoin Cash), to make a quick buck,leveraging of power within mining communities and other nefarious motivations.
With a 5 algo crypto, the community is even more diversified. This system tends to cause a larger distribution of the coin and mining power to different communities with different views and opinions. It is less likely that all miners from these different pools will join together to leverage power against others. A reduction in the risk of 51% attacks is another positive side effect.

Marketing, Branding and Community.
Major Crypto gives +2 on our 1 to 3 scale. Its logo has a great modern design. and a memorable slogan. “A coin for everyone” which is tied to the word myriad meaning “a countless or extremely great number.” attesting to its desire to reach as many people as they can.

They additionally have a very strong community that is quick and helpful in their responses on reddit and twitter. They give free stickers to their followers. But be patient they are in high demand.






Tech


As for tech, there multi-algo is understated in the crypto community. One of the biggest problems in the cryptocurrency world is centralized power which sways the value of currencies, reduces stability and gives a bad reputation. They are still in their infant stages in comparison to the larger market. But they have a solid vision for the future as a coin of mass adoption. They are dedicated to a proof of work approach with a potential for a proof of stake chain in the future. They reference the issues currently had with larger cryptocurrencies such as bitcoin in their motivation for how they evolve Myriad Coin. 










Adoption 


They want to focus on scaling and building a community for 2018. Multi algo is still relatively new to the world of crypto, and regardless of its potential utility, it is not a high priority to the overall community. Maybe because they just don’t know what’s good for them. Maybe because there isn't a desire to adopt it. With Digitbyte (one of Myriads close relatives) Accelerating up the charts as a multi algo, Myriad Coin has some obstacles to overcome as being THE multi algo for the people. We give Myriadcoin a median score of 2 for tech.


When I asked the community about their views on adoption, they said they were focused on adoption with their potential users and more exchanges. They believed that merchant adoption was not the key to success at this stage in their development. Reddit user Myriad_Angel told us that, adoption in the early stages is overrated when one considers the importance of making a cryptocurrency fully functional. They cited that bitcoin is currently losing merchants and no one is complaining. They concerns even at the top of the blockchain have little to do with the currencies being used, well, as currencies. At least not yet. We rate adoption at 1 considering their listings on Bittrex and Cryptopia.
